In the current scenario, research into the creation of renewable energy from organic resources and waste has benefited from the excessive use of conventional fuels and the influence of greenhouse levels in the atmosphere. The majority of the energy used worldwide is produced from fossil fuels due to the enormous demand for energy. Anaerobic digestion (AD), which combines the creation of biofuel and environmentally friendly waste management, is referred to as an alternative and effective technique in recent studies.
