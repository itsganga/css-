ZnO nanoparticles were obtained using the sol-gel method. Analysis of XRD and FTIR data revealed that XRD exhibited the characteristic spherical shape structure. The results from SEM and XRD indicated that the peaks are obtained as standard peaks and the size range lies between 100-150nm. Based on the reaction and the obtained results, it was observed that starch plays role of a stabilizer and polymerizing agent for the large-scale preparation of ZnO-NPs using the sol-gel method. Overall, this study demonstrates the viability of the sol-gel method with starch as a promising approach for the synthesis of ZnO-NPs with desired characteristics. Further research can explore the applications of these synthesized ZnO-NPs in various such as optoelectronics, catalysis, and biomedical applications, considering their well-defined structure and controlled properties.
